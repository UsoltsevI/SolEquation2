If (argv[1] == "--test") start_s runs sol_test() to test program. Then main() passes the coefficients to the solve_qe(...) function and tells her the address of the roots structure, designed to store roots and their number. After main() sends the roots structure to the output(...) function, which outputs the received values to the screen. This is followed by the completion of the main() function
